High Impact Apr 2, 2026 via CISA.gov
CISA releases updated SLTT Cyber Resilience guidance — mandatory DR testing for critical infra
State, Local, Tribal, and Territorial orgs now required to conduct documented DR exercises twice annually. Non-compliance affects federal grant eligibility. Plain english: your SLED prospects just got a compliance mandate they have to document.
Watch Mar 31, 2026 via SEC.gov
SEC clarifies 4-day cyber disclosure rule — backup failure counts as "material incident"
New SEC guidance explicitly states that backup failure resulting in data unavailability qualifies as a material cybersecurity incident requiring 8-K disclosure within 4 business days. This is new. Public company prospects need to know this changes their calculus on "we'll deal with it later."
